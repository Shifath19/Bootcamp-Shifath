The Criteria I followed for reviewing the designs
  1. Readability: . How the content presented is styled for better readability. Includes fonts, spacing, colors and themes.
  2. Extent to which they follwed the Guidelines: How the functionality or design is implemented compared to the actual rules mentioned in the docs.
  3. Layout: How the information is presented and strucutured.
  4. Consistency: Whether overall design is consistent in each and every page and divisions.
  5. Responsiveness:Whether the webpages are compatible for different viewports.
